planning of project

Planning a software project involves setting up the groundwork for a successful development process. It includes defining objectives, determining resources, scheduling tasks, and identifying potential risks. Effective planning is essential for delivering a project on time, within budget, and meeting the desired quality standards.

Project Planning

Requirement Analysis

Define the project's scope and gather detailed requirements. Determine functional and non-functional requirements. Identify stakeholders and their needs.

Feasibility Study

Evaluate the technical, operational, and financial feasibility. Conduct cost-benefit analysis. Identify potential risks and challenges.
